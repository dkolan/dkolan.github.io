Andrew College Alma Mater

Where the spires of old Cuthbert pierece the southern blue,

Proudly stands our Alma Mater, Noble, grand and true.

Blue and gold float forever, Bright our colors shine!

Hail to thee, our Alma Mater! Hail to Andrew mine!


As our classic halls of learning so thy worth appears,

And thy mem'ry still shall linger, Thru life's smiles and tears.

Lift the chorus, wake the echoes, Make the welkin ring!

Hail the queen of all the southland! Loud her praises sing!
